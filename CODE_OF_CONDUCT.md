# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors, maintainers, and community members pledge to make participation in ApiForge a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

Regardless of:

* Age
* Body size
* Disability
* Ethnicity
* Gender identity
* Nationality
* Race
* Religion
* Sexual identity
* Level of experience

Everyone is welcome to contribute.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

* Being respectful and professional
* Accepting constructive feedback
* Collaborating effectively
* Helping new contributors
* Sharing knowledge openly
* Focusing on project goals

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples include:

* Harassment or discrimination
* Personal attacks
* Trolling
* Insults or derogatory language
* Public or private intimidation
* Publishing private information without permission
* Any other conduct considered inappropriate

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing acceptable behavior standards.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within:

* GitHub Issues
* Pull Requests
* Discussions
* Project Documentation
* Community Spaces

It also applies when representing ApiForge in public spaces.

---

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated fairly.
